What is this?

CoinFlip is a webpage for settling scores.
If you've ever had a discussion with your friend, spouse or dog of what to do, where to eat, or where to take a walk and simply not being able to make unanimous a decision? Well look no further!

CoinFlip will make the decision for you Below is a description of the different pages.

Guess

On this page you can play a guessing game where you enter an arbitrary number of coins and then your job is to guess the amount of flips needed to get an alternating sequence of coins. E.g. the sequence [1, 1, 1] would need 1 flip to become alternating: [1, 0, 1]

Flip

Flip a coin.
On this page you pick heads or tails and then flip it for a reliable randomized result!
