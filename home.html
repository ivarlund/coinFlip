What is this?

CoinFlip is a webpage for settling scores or friendly disputes.
Ever had a discussion with your friend, spouse or dog of what to do, where to eat, or where to take a walk and simply not being able to make unanimous a decision? Well look no further!

At CoinFlip you can let a website make the decision for you! Through our advanced scientifically and field tested coin-flipping systems you can impartially make decisions.

Below is a description of the different pages.

Guess

Need to decide wether to buy that new TV? Or how about that fancy computer you've been looking at and probably will buy no matter what the outcome is here? Well here is your way of removing all claims of responsibility.
On this page you can play a guessing game where you have to guess the amount of flips needed to get an alternating sequence of coins.

Flip

Need to sell your house? Flip a coin. Quit your job? Flip a coin. Move to another country? Flip a coin.
On this page you can pick a coin side and then flip it for a reliable, responsible result!
