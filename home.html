What is this?

CoinFlip is a webpage for settling scores.
Ever had a discussion with your friend, spouse or dog of what to do, where to eat, or where to take a walk and simply not being able to make unanimous a decision? Well look no further!

At CoinFlip you can let a website make the decision for you! Through our advanced scientifically and field tested coin-flipping systems you can impartially make decisions.

Below is a description of the different pages.

Guess

Here is your way of removing all claims of responsibility when making hard decisions.
On this page you can play a guessing game where you enter an arbitrary number of coins and then have to guess the amount of flips needed to get an alternating sequence of coins.

Flip

Thinking of selling your house? Quitting your job? Moving to another country? Flip a coin.
On this page you can pick a coin side and then flip it for a reliable and responsible result!
